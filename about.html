Our Story

Baker’s Paradise is a family-owned business in the center of Jacksonville Beach, since 2014. Our story began with a simple love for baking. We started out small, baking for our friends and family. Soon, word of our delicious treats spread, and we opened our doors to the public. We have been growing ever since, but we have never forgotten our commitment to quality and taste.

We use only the freshest ingredients in our products. We source our flour from local farms, our eggs from free-range chickens, and our milk from grass-fed cows. We also make everything from scratch in our own kitchen..
